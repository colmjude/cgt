Cross-government tools for civil servants

Many of the tools civil servants use today no longer meet the high standards we set for external-facing services.

Sir Jeremy Heywood and Departmental Digital Leaders want our internal-facing services to meet these standards, with user needs at the core.

What have we done?

Over a 12-week discovery phase we:

  • identified the user needs of civil servants
  • mapped those needs against existing tools and services in areas like recruitment, HR and learning
  • identified barriers to building high-quality services
  • built a prototype showing what world-class digital services for civil servants could look like
