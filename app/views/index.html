Cross-government tools for Civil Servants

Many of the tools civil servants use today no longer meet the high standards we set for external-facing services.

Sir Jeremy Heywood and Departmental Digital Leaders have agreed we need to ensure our internal-facing services meet these standards, with user needs at the core.

What are we doing?

This is a 12-week discovery phase and we aim to:-

  • understand the needs of civil servants
  • map those needs against existing tools and services in areas like recruitment, HR and learning
  • identify barriers to building high-quality services
  • prototype what world-class digital services for civil servants could look like
